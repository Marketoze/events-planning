Frequently Asked Questions

What types of events do you organize in Faisalabad?

We organize all types of events including birthday parties, corporate events, weddings, mehndi, baraat, walima, anniversary celebrations, themed parties, outdoor events, product launches, conferences, seminars, team building events, and more. We serve Faisalabad and all surrounding areas with our comprehensive event planning services.

How far in advance should I book your services?

We recommend booking at least 2-4 weeks in advance for smaller events like birthday parties, and 2-3 months in advance for larger events like weddings. However, we understand that sometimes events are planned on short notice, and we do our best to accommodate last-minute bookings based on availability.

What is included in your event packages?

Our packages typically include decoration, sound system, lighting, furniture setup, and event coordination. Premium packages add photography, catering, entertainment, and more. All packages are customizable based on your specific needs and budget. Contact us for a detailed breakdown tailored to your event.

Do you provide catering services?

Yes! We offer comprehensive catering services for all types of events. Our menu options range from traditional Pakistani cuisine to international dishes. We can accommodate various dietary requirements and customize menus based on your preferences and guest count.

What is your pricing structure?

Our pricing varies based on event type, guest count, services required, and customization level. Basic packages start from PKR 25,000, Premium packages from PKR 75,000, and Luxury packages from PKR 150,000. We provide free consultations and customized quotes to match your specific requirements and budget.

Do you handle venue booking as well?

Absolutely! We can help you find and book the perfect venue for your event. We have partnerships with various venues across Faisalabad including banquet halls, outdoor locations, hotels, and farmhouses. We'll help you select a venue that fits your vision and budget.

Can you create custom themes for parties?

Yes! Custom themed parties are our specialty. Whether you want a superhero theme, princess party, vintage elegance, tropical paradise, or any unique concept, our creative team can bring your vision to life with custom decorations, props, costumes, and themed activities.
